Um livro de poesia

Title: Um livro de poesia (A book of poetry)
Author: Diogo Nunes
Pages: 108
Published: March 2010

 (read  times)

Synopsis:
I'm a young man. Student. Thoughtful. Amateur poet.
This book contains poems about emotions and reflections of my life. With this book I want two things: to proof that interesting poetry does not have to be indecipherable to most people and to show young kids that reading is good for them because books enhance their creativity and thinking. I want to inspire the youth through my example, since I started hating books and now I'm writing them! I want to make the reader feel and think about what I wrote.
